Minimum Requirements

Meeting the following criteria helps ensure you’ll qualify for funding. If you’re close but not quite there, feel free to apply—we consider every case individually.

  • At least 6 months in business
  • Annual revenue of $100,000 or more
  • An active business checking account
  • FICO score of 625 or higher

Don’t meet one of these requirements? Contact us to discuss alternative options. We’re committed to finding a path forward.
