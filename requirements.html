Minimum Requirements

Visa Capital strives to help a wide variety of businesses get funded. To qualify, please ensure that your business meets the following criteria:

  • At least 6 months in business
  • Annual revenue of $100K or more
  • An active business checking account
  • FICO® score of 625 or higher

If you meet these requirements, you’re well on your way to getting the funding you need. Get Started
