FAQ

Questions, answered.

Don't see yours? Email our team — we usually reply within an hour.

How fast can I really get funded? +

Most decisions happen the same day you apply. Once you accept terms and sign electronically, funds typically wire to your business account within 24 to 48 hours.

Will applying hurt my credit score? +

No. We use a soft credit pull during prequalification, which has zero impact on your personal credit score. A hard pull only happens if you decide to move forward with a specific offer.

What are the minimum requirements? +

At least six months in business, $100,000+ in annual revenue, an active business checking account, and a FICO score of 625 or higher. Close but not quite? Apply anyway — we look at the whole picture.

How are your rates calculated? +

Rates depend on your time in business, monthly revenue, industry, and the product you choose. We always disclose the all-in cost up front — no hidden fees, no fine print, no surprises.

Do I need collateral? +

Most of our products are unsecured, meaning no specific collateral is pledged. Equipment financing uses the equipment itself as collateral, and SBA loans may require a personal guarantee.

Can I pay off my loan early? +

Yes. Most of our products allow early payoff with no prepayment penalty, and several offer discounts for paying off ahead of schedule. Your specialist will walk you through specifics.
