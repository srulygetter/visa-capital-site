Our Funding Process

We’ve designed our application and funding process to be transparent and efficient. Here’s what to expect when you work with Visa Capital:

1

Apply Online

Submit a quick application online in minutes. Provide basic information about your business, revenue and funding needs. There’s no obligation and no hard credit pull.

2

Get a Decision

Our underwriters will review your application and contact you to discuss your options. Many decisions are made the same day, with clear terms and no hidden fees.

3

Receive Your Funds

Once approved, funds are wired to your business bank account as soon as the next business day. Put the capital to work immediately and grow your business.

Our team guides you through each step and remains available for any questions. Ready to get started?
