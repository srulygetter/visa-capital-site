Our Process

Getting funding from Visa Capital is simple and hassle‑free. Follow our three easy steps and get the capital you need, when you need it.

1. Apply

Complete our quick online application in minutes. We require basic information about your business such as revenue, time in business and funding needs. No lengthy paperwork or endless documentation.

2. Get a Decision

Our funding specialists will review your information and tailor financing options that fit your unique situation. We make decisions fast, often within hours, and there’s no hard credit pull to check your eligibility.

3. Receive Your Funds

Once approved, you can receive funds in your bank account as soon as the same day. Use your capital to take advantage of opportunities, buy equipment or smooth out cash flow.
