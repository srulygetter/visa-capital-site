Our Financing Solutions

Select from a range of flexible funding options designed to support the unique needs of your business. Click each item to learn more.

Merchant Cash Advance

Receive up to $600K based on your future credit and debit card sales. Remittances are tied directly to your revenue so your payments flex with your cash flow. Perfect for businesses that need working capital fast without fixed monthly payments.

Business Line of Credit

Get revolving credit limits between $10K and $200K. Draw funds as you need them and only pay interest on what you use. Our lines of credit come with flexible repayment terms of up to 24 months to help you manage cash flow and unexpected expenses.

Term Loans

Borrow $10K to $400K with clear, fixed repayment schedules up to 24 months. Term loans are ideal for larger investments like facility expansion, new hires or major marketing campaigns. Enjoy predictable payments and no prepayment penalties.

Equipment Financing

Finance or lease essential equipment up to $150K while preserving your working capital. Our equipment financing options come with competitive rates and flexible end-of-term choices, so you can upgrade, purchase or return your equipment as needed.
